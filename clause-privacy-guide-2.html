Google

MOST COMPLEX

Follow the glowing line top to bottom. Every step is one tap in your Google Account.

STEP 01
Open your privacy dashboard
myaccount.google.com → Data & Privacy ↗

This one screen controls almost everything below. Everything happens from here.

STEP 02
Stop search & app tracking
Web & App Activity ↗

Toggle it off, or tap "Auto-delete" and set it to 3 months so old history clears itself.

STEP 03
Erase existing search history
My Activity → Delete → All time ↗

This wipes what Google already logged, not just what happens going forward.

STEP 04
Turn off location tracking
Location History (Timeline) ↗

Turn off, or set auto-delete to 3 months. This is the log of everywhere your phone has been.

STEP 05
Clear YouTube activity
YouTube History ↗

Pause watch & search history, then set auto-delete to 3 months.

STEP 06
Turn off ad personalization
Ad Settings → Ad Personalization ↗

Stops Google from building an interest profile out of your activity to target ads.

STEP 07
Review connected apps
Third-party Apps with Account Access ↗

Remove anything you don't recognize or no longer use — each one is a door into your account.

STEP 08
Turn on 2-Step Verification
Security → 2-Step Verification ↗

Single biggest lock you can add. Stops account access even if your password leaks.

STEP 09
Check who's logged into your account
Device Activity ↗

Shows every device currently signed in. Sign out anything you don't recognize.

STEP 10
Remove yourself from Google Search results
Results About You ↗

Ask Google to remove your phone number, address, or personal info surfacing in search — and get notified if new info about you appears.

WhatsApp

SPAM & PROMO

Every promotional / business message you didn't ask for goes through these three doors.

STEP 01
Block message floods from unknown numbers
Settings → Privacy → Advanced → Block Unknown Account Messages ↗

Auto-blocks unknown numbers that send a high volume of messages in a short time — the exact pattern promo/spam accounts use.

STEP 02
Opt out of a specific business
Open the business chat → Block/Report ↗

Verified business chats have a built-in "stop promotional messages" option — no need to block the whole contact if you still want order updates.

STEP 03
Silence unknown callers
Settings → Privacy → Calls ↗

Stops spam/scam calls from ringing your phone entirely — they land silently in a call log instead.

Gmail

SUBSCRIPTIONS & PROMO

The goal: only mail you're actually waiting for hits your main inbox.

STEP 01
Turn on the Promotions tab
Settings → Inbox → Categories ↗

Every marketing/subscription email routes to its own tab automatically, away from real mail — no filters needed.

STEP 02
Unsubscribe in bulk
Filters & Blocked Addresses ↗

Gmail shows an "Unsubscribe" link next to the sender on most promo mail now — one tap kills it at the source instead of just hiding it.

STEP 03
Auto-delete repeat senders
Create Filter → Delete It ↗

For senders that ignore unsubscribe requests, a filter rule sends every future email from them straight to Trash on arrival.
